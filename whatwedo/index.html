What We Do

CISxIdeas creates free and inclusive opportunities in practical, innovative STEM learning in the form of hackathons, workshops, and challenges.

How are we inclusive?

All of CISxIdeas’s events are accessible to students around the world, regardless of their race, socioeconomic background, or gender.

How is the learning we promote practical and innovative?

CISxIdeas makes learning innovative and practical through connecting the skills in STEM with addressing SDGs and global issues, providing inspirations from experts and opportunities to apply STEM knowledge in events. Through this approach to STEM, CISxIdeas fosters skills of global problem solving capable of addressing all SDGs, such as climate action and reduced inequalities.

Potential Impact

  • Connect student innovators with active changemakers in governments and industries with more resources, networks, and experience
  • Magnify the impact of students’ ideas and rising projects
  • Potentially improve the accessibility of technology to underprivileged communities through workshops and educational activities
